# CIT Events Portal

## About CIT
Chennai Institute of Technology (CIT) is a premier educational institution located in Tamilnadu, committed to fostering innovation, creativity, and academic excellence among its students.

## Overview
The CIT Events Portal is a comprehensive platform designed to bridge the gap between event organizers and student participants. It serves as a centralized hub for discovering, managing, and participating in various college events, workshops, hackathons, and academic programs.

## Features

### For Students
- **Discover Events**: Browse through a wide variety of events, workshops, hackathons, and programs
- **Easy Registration**: Register for events with just a few clicks
- **Event Details**: Access comprehensive information about dates, timings, venues, and descriptions
- **Track Participation**: Keep track of events you've registered for and attended

### For Organizers
- **Create Events**: Easily organize and manage events, workshops, hackathons, and programs
- **Manage Registrations**: Track and manage student registrations and participation
- **Event Promotion**: Reach out to a large audience of interested students
- **Analytics**: View event statistics and participant feedback

## Event Categories
- **Workshops**: Technical and skill-development workshops conducted by industry experts
- **Hackathons**: Innovation-driven programming competitions and problem-solving events
- **Academic Programs**: Seminars, talks, and certification programs
- **Cultural Events**: Festivals, competitions, and social gatherings
- **Technical Talks**: Lectures and sessions by professionals and alumni

## How to Get Started
1. Visit the CIT Events Portal
2. Create an account or log in
3. For Students: Browse events and register for your interests
4. For Organizers: Create an event and invite students to participate

## Contact
For queries and support, please contact the student affairs office or event management team.
